Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli has achieved remarkable success and set numerous records in international cricket.

Early Life and Career

Virat Kohli showed exceptional talent from a young age and represented Delhi in various age-group levels before making his debut for India in 2008. His passion and determination were evident early on, and he quickly became a key player in the Indian team.

International Career

Kohli's international career has been nothing short of phenomenal. He has amassed thousands of runs across all formats of the game and holds several records, including the fastest century by an Indian cricketer in ODIs.

Achievements

1.ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018)
2.Leading Run-scorer for India in T20Is
3.Fastest Batsman to Score 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 Runs in ODIs

Leadership

Virat Kohli took over the captaincy of the Indian cricket team in 2017 and has led the team with passion and intensity. Under his captaincy, India has achieved numerous victories, including historic series wins abroad.
